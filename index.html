陈淑敏，南京大学新闻传播学院2022级硕士研究生。

研究兴趣集中于数字媒体和流行文化中的传播现象和人类行为，包括数据新闻的生产、电子游戏中的社会关系、网络小说的偏见影响等。曾在核心期刊上发表过3篇相关学术论文。

I'm a master student in School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University.
My research interest focuses on the communication phenomena and human behaviors in digital media and popular culture, including the production of data news, social relations in electronic games, and the prejudice influence of online novels. I have published 3 related academic papers in core journals.
